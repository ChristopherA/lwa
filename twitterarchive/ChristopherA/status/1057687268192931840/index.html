One of my favorite cooperative board games with a horror theme is “Last Friday”. It is asymmetric—one player plays an invisible summer camp slasher, who alternatively hunts the campers during the night phase or is hunted by the campers during the day. Fun! https://www.google.com/amp/s/arstechnica.com/gaming/2017/05/last-friday-the-board-game-in-which-you-play-a-machete-wielding-maniac/
