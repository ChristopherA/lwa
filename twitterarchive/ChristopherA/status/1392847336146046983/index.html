The first is ByteWords. This is a way to encode binary data (such as cryptographic seeds) as easy-to-recognize, hard-to-confuse words. Bytewords are also well-integrated with QRs. [7/14] https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RYgOFSdUqWY#t=16m21s
